The Multiplier
MartinLoop360 — Agents That Buy Governance for Agents
MartinLoop360 · Design Preview · v1.3.0
The world's first agent-native commerce layer for AI governance.
MartinLoop360 is the protocol layer that enables autonomous AI agents — not humans — to discover, quote, purchase, and run Martin Loop governance on behalf of the AI-native corporations they operate within. An agent can call martin360_quote, receive a signed mandate, execute a governed run, and receive a cryptographically signed receipt — all autonomously, with no human in the loop. This is governance infrastructure for the age of autonomous corporations.
